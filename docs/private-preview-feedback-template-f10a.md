# FeelFlick private preview — feedback form

> A short form for preview testers. Copy it into a Google Form / Typeform, or just reply
> in your own words — whatever's easiest. **Honest "it didn't land / I didn't trust it"
> is the most useful thing you can give us.** Fill it after a night or two of real use;
> better still, send one for **each night** you use it (different moods = the best signal).

---

## About this session

1. **What day/night and roughly what time** did you open FeelFlick?
2. **What mood were you in** when you opened it? (in your own words — tired, restless,
   wired, melancholy, want-to-be-moved, background-noise, etc.)
3. **How well did the app know you yet?** (first night / a few films logged / it's got a
   good sense of me)

## Tonight's pick

4. **What was tonight's pick?** (title, if you remember)
5. **Did the pick feel relevant** to your mood + taste? (1 = not at all → 5 = exactly right)
6. **Did the "Why this pick" reason help?** Did it make you trust the pick more — or feel
   generic / overconfident / off? (quote it if you can)
7. **What did you do with it** — open, save, skip, or watch? **And why?** (the "why" is
   the important part — especially if you skipped)
8. If you **skipped**: what would the *right* pick have been, or what was wrong with this one?

## Trust & honesty

9. **Did the Film File** (the film's page / the case for it) make the recommendation feel
   **more trustworthy**? Why / why not?
10. **Did Cinematic DNA** (your taste profile) feel **honest**, or **generic / made-up**?
11. **Did anything feel fake, overconfident, or like too much?** (fake reviews, inflated
    claims, a "why" that overpromised, numbers you didn't believe) — this one matters a lot.

## The ritual

12. **Where did you feel confused** at any point? (what were you trying to do?)
13. **Would you come back tomorrow?** (yes / maybe / no) — and **what would make you**?
14. Anything else — what you loved, what annoyed you, what you wish it did.

> Please **don't** worry about pricing, "missing features," TV/series, or comparisons to
> other apps right now — we're only testing whether the **single nightly pick earns your
> trust**. If you have ideas there, jot them at the end and we'll save them for later.

# Welcome to the FeelFlick private preview 🎬

Thanks for trying FeelFlick early. This is a small, invite-only preview — you're one
of a handful of people seeing it. Please **don't share it publicly or post screenshots**
yet; it's still being polished.

This guide is short on purpose. There's no wrong way to use FeelFlick — just use it the
way you'd actually pick a film for tonight, and tell us how it felt.

---

## What FeelFlick is

Most apps give you an endless grid and make *you* do the work of choosing. FeelFlick
does the opposite: you tell it **how you feel**, and it gives you **one** film for
tonight — tuned to your mood and everything you've loved on screen — and it tells you
**why** it's the one.

One considered pick a night. Not a feed to scroll. The goal is a recommendation you can
**trust**.

(Movies only for now — no TV/series yet.)

---

## Try this first (about 5 minutes)

1. **Open the app** and **sign in** (see below).
2. **Do the quick onboarding** — tell it a few films you love. This seeds your taste.
3. Go to **Tonight** and look at your pick. Read the **"Why this pick"** line.
4. Decide as you naturally would: **open it**, **save it for later**, or **skip it**.
5. Open the **Film File** for the pick (tap the poster / "See More") and read the case
   for it.
6. Peek at your **Cinematic DNA** (your taste profile) on **Profile**.
7. Come back **tomorrow** and see your next pick. (Coming back across a few different
   nights/moods is the most useful thing you can do.)

## How to do each thing

### Sign in
- Use the link we sent you to open the app, then **sign in with Google**. That's it —
  no password to create.
- First time in, you'll land on a short **onboarding**. Returning visits drop you
  straight on **Tonight**.

### Onboarding (first time)
- Pick or search a few films you genuinely love. Be honest — this is what your
  recommendations are built from.
- If it asks *why* it's asking for something, that's intentional — we try to never
  collect anything without telling you what it's for.

### Tonight (your nightly pick)
- This is the heart of FeelFlick: **one pick for tonight**, with a short **"Why this
  pick"** reason.
- You can set/adjust your **mood** — try opening it in different moods on different
  nights and see if the pick changes in a way that makes sense.
- Actions on the pick:
  - **Open / See More** — go to the film's full page (the Film File).
  - **Save** — keep it for later (adds it to your Queue).
  - **Skip** — not tonight. Skipping is useful — it teaches the app, it's not a failure.
  - **Mark watched** — if you've seen it (or after you watch it).

### Film File (the case for a film)
- The film's own page makes the **case** for why it might be your pick — the editorial
  take, mood fit, and notes. This is where a pick should start feeling trustworthy (or not).
- Any "notes" you see are **editorial/illustrative, not real user reviews** — we label
  them honestly and never fake reviews or ratings.

### Profile / Cinematic DNA
- Your **DNA** is your taste made visible. Early on it will say it has only a little
  evidence about you — that's deliberate honesty, not a bug. It should get richer as you
  log more films. Tell us if it ever feels **generic or made-up**.

## What feedback helps us most

We'll send you a short [feedback form](private-preview-feedback-template-f10a.md), but
in your own words, the gold is:

- What **mood** were you in, and did **tonight's pick fit** it?
- Did the **"Why this pick"** reason actually help you trust it?
- Did you **save / open / skip / watch** — and *why*?
- Did the **Film File** make the pick feel more trustworthy?
- Did your **Cinematic DNA** feel **honest** or generic?
- Where did you get **confused**?
- Did anything feel **fake, overconfident, or like too much**?
- What would make you **come back tomorrow**?

Honest "this didn't land / I didn't trust it" is **more useful** than polite praise.
Don't worry about feature requests or pricing right now — we just want to know if the
nightly pick earns your trust.

## Known limitations (so they don't surprise you)

- **Movies only** — no TV/series surfaces yet.
- **Cold start is thin** — your first night or two, the app knows little about you, so
  the pick is more of a starting guess. It improves as you log films. (That's exactly
  the moment we most want your honest read on.)
- **Small catalog corners** — some niche/very-new films may be missing.
- A few surfaces are still being refined; rough edges are expected — that's why you're
  here. If something looks broken, that's a great thing to report.

---

## Bugs

If something breaks (a page won't load, a button does nothing, something looks wrong),
note it in the feedback form's **bug section** — the page/route you were on, your
device/browser, what happened vs. what you expected, and a screenshot if it's easy.

---

## A note on privacy

- We use what you do in the app — the films you **save, skip, watch, rate**, and your
  **mood** — to tune *your* recommendations. That's the whole point of the single pick.
- We use basic, privacy-respecting **error monitoring** to catch crashes; session replays
  **mask text** so we're not reading your content.
- This is a **private preview** — please **don't share the link, post screenshots, or
  talk about specifics publicly** until we say it's ready.
- Want your data removed after the preview? Just ask — we'll take care of it.

Thank you — genuinely. One honest reaction from you is worth more than a hundred
anonymous clicks. 🙏
